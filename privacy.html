Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 11, 2026

Overview

Toolly is designed as a directory experience with a strong privacy-first approach. We aim to keep data collection minimal and transparent.

What We Collect

Toolly does not require account creation for browsing and searching tools. Core filtering and search interactions are performed in your browser.

When you use features like "My Tools," preferences may be stored in your browser local storage on your device.

What We Do Not Do

We do not sell your personal data and do not require personal profiles to use the directory.
