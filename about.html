Your Privacy Matters

All browsing and search operations happen locally in your browser. When you search for tools or filter by category on Toolly, everything runs on your device. Your search queries, browsing history, and tool preferences are never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

What We Do:

  • Provide a static, fast-loading directory of AI tools
  • Run all search and filtering operations in your browser
  • Use localStorage only for your personal tool preferences (stays on your device)
  • Provide direct links to official tool websites

What We DON'T Do:

  • Track your searches or browsing behavior
  • Store any personal information on our servers
  • Sell or share your data with third parties
  • Require account creation or login
  • Use intrusive analytics or tracking cookies
100% Privacy-Focused

How It Works

Toolly is designed to be intuitive and efficient. Whether you're looking for a specific type of AI tool or just browsing for inspiration, we make it easy to find what you need.

1

Browse Categories

Explore 15+ categories including Chatbots, Image Generation, Video Tools, Developer Tools, and more. Click the quick category pills in the hero section or use the sidebar for detailed categories.

2

Search & Filter

Use the search bar to find tools by name, description, or tags. Filter by category, sort by name or featured status, and switch between grid or list view for your preferred browsing experience.

3

Compare Tools

Read detailed descriptions, check badges (Featured, Trending, Free, Freemium, Paid), and view tags to understand each tool's capabilities. All information is curated and regularly updated.

4

Visit & Try

Click "Visit Site" on any tool card to open the official website in a new tab. Test the tool directly from the source - we provide direct links to ensure you always get the latest version.

Pro Tip

Use the "My Tools" section to save your favorite tools for quick access. You can add custom tools, organize them with drag-and-drop, and even auto-fetch logos from any website!
